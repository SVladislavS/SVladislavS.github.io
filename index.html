How to progress?

Just a few simple steps

  • Daily planner
  • Reminder
  • Wake up early
  • Limit time before TV

1

Adhere to principle 70/30

The best solution is to plan 70% of your own time. You must admit that some events are difficult to predict, and almost every day a certain “surprise effect” occurs, so you should always leave some of the time free.

2

Make a plan the next day

Separate cases by importance by dividing the notebook into two columns. In the first, write down what needs to be done immediately. In the second - which is less important and in case of force majeure it can be rescheduled to another day.

3

Create realistic planning

Do not overwhelm yourself with such a volume of work that you cannot handle. Do not go to extremes of excessive planning (like any mountains on your shoulder) and plan only such a volume of tasks that you can really cope with.
